2016: Acquisition of treasury shares in the context of the LTIP 2012

The acquisition of treasury shares launched on March 16, 2016 has been concluded on March 16, 2016. A total of 26,047 shares have been acquired at an average price of Euro 129.3405. The total price amounted to Euro 3,368,932.00.

The shares will be used to fulfill the rights of entitled parties, including the Executive Board members, to receive shares in Linde Aktiengesellschaft as part of the Share Matching Plan. The Share Matching Plan is part of the Long Term Incentive Plan (LTIP) 2012 which has been approved by the shareholders meeting on May 4, 2012 under item 8 of the agenda.
