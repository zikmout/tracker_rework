Share buy-back programs

ISIN DE0008404005 / WKN 840400

Allianz executed four share buy-back programs with an aggregate volume of 7.5 billion euros in the period from February 17, 2017 to July 30, 2019. A total number of 39,827,141 shares were repurchased and cancelled. The number of issued shares was thereby reduced from 457,000,000 (as of December 31, 2016) to 417,172,859 (effective on November 11, 2019).

Share buy-back I

On February 16, 2017 the Board of Management of Allianz SE decided on a share buy-back with a volume of up to 3 billion euros. The share buy-back was executed in the period from February 17 until December 15, 2017.

Overall, 16,750,354 shares have been canceled, thereof 7,472,978 shares in the first tranche (effective on June 27, 2017), 3,425,722 in the second tranche (effective on September 19, 2017) and 5,851,654 in the third tranche (effective on December 20, 2017). The number of issued shares was thereby reduced from 457,000,000 to 440,249,646.
