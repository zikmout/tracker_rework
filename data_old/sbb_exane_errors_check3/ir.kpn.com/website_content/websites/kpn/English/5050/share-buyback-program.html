Share Buyback Program

KPN has completed the EUR 200m share buyback program announced on 26 April 2017. The program started on 10 May 2017 and has been completed on 10 August 2017. The purpose of the program was to reduce the capital of KPN.

Update 10 August 2017

KPN announces that, from 7 August to 10 August 2017, it has repurchased 4,648,960 KPN ordinary shares at an average price of EUR 3.12 on Euronext Amsterdam. During the share buyback program, 67,410,260 ordinary shares have been repurchased at an average price of 2.97. This completes the EUR 200m share buyback program.
